CONTROLS

W or ↑ - move forward
S or ↓ - move backwards

C - fly down

Page Up - look up
Page Down - look down

Right click - open menu
Left click - Place object
Z and U - rotate left and right
K and M - move up and down
J and N - move farther and closer
H and B - bigger and smaller
