CONTROLS

W or ↑ - move forward
S or ↓ - move backwards
A or ← - turn left
D or → - turn right
Drag mouse to look around

Shift - run

Spacebar - fly up
C - fly down

Page Up - look up
Page Down - look down

Right click - open menu

Enter - Place object

R and T - rotate Y
Z and U - rotate X
I and O - rotate Z
K and M - move up and down
J and N - move farther and closer
H and B - bigger and smaller

CTRL + MouseDrag - rotate the object
